Your Documentation Home Page

Every documentationproject has a home page, whihc is the default page that appears at the root of the URL path. This is the home page for the docuTree project.

This page is the index.htm file within the home folder in the docutree filesystem. It is pointed to by the redirect tag that sits in the index.htm at the top level of your docutree folder.

Customizing this home page to describe your project, would be one of the first things you will want to do after understanding the concepts covered in the About docuTree page and the Page Template and Style Guide.
